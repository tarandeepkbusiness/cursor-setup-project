3. Improve page experience
4. Optimize on-page SEO
5. Fix technical SEO issues
6. Build quality backlinks
7. Optimize for AI Overviews
8. Fix keyword cannibalization
9. Demonstrate E-E-A-T

Now imagine what happens next

That one post can:

↳ Rank in Google for SEO topics
↳ Appear in LinkedIn search
↳ Get shared by other marketers
↳ Be cited by AI if it provides useful, original insights

One post

Multiple discovery channels 💱

Quick FAQ

Q: Do social posts help SEO?
Yes. They can increase visibility, earn brand mentions, attract backlinks, and even appear in Google search results

Q: Can ChatGPT or Google AI use LinkedIn content?
Yes. If the content is public, helpful, and trusted, it can become part of the information AI systems use

Q: Should I stop publishing blogs?

No. Your website is still your foundation

Winning strategy is:

Website + LinkedIn + YouTube + other trusted platforms
