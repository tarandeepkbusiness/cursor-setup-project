# Research Summary: Cam Trew
**Topic:** AI-Driven Business Workflows and Data Compounding

## 1. The 3 Layers of AI Business
Cam argues that every AI-powered strategy must be built in this specific order:
1. **Data:** The foundation. High-quality output starts with high-quality input.
2. **Media:** Distribution is the only moat. You can copy a product, but you can't copy an audience.
3. **Product/Service:** Using data to deliver exactly what the audience wants.

## 2. The "Autopilot" Lead Gen & Sales Playbook
Cam provides a technical stack for B2B SaaS growth:
- **Lead Gen:** Apollo + Instantly for automated cold outreach.
- **Sales Optimization:** Recording calls with Fathom -> Dropping transcripts into NotebookLM to identify where deals are being lost.
- **Brand Voice:** Training Claude on personal tone/style to ensure AI content sounds human.

## 3. Product-Market Fit via AI
- **Roadmap Automation:** Instead of guessing what to build, paste customer call transcripts into Claude to find recurring pain points. Build what people *ask* for, not what you *think* they want.
