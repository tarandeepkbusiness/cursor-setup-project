Everyone's building an AI company. But most don't realise they're actually in the data business.

Here are the 3 layers every AI business is built on:

1. Data

- Every AI model runs on data
- User behaviour tells you what to build next
- Data turns assumptions into decisions
- Data compounds over time
- The quality of your output starts with your data quality

2. Media

- A good product means nothing without distribution
- Your product can be copied, but your audience can't
- Good content works harder than any sales team
- Paid ads stop the moment you stop paying
- People buy from brands they trust

3. Product/Service

- This is where everything comes together
- Use your data to deliver what people actually want
- Your audience will leave if the product doesn't deliver
- Retention starts and ends here
- Happy customers become your best case studies

Most businesses get this order wrong.

They build the product first, figure out distribution later, then never think about the data.
