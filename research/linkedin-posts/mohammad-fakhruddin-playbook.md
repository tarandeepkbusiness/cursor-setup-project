# Research Summary: Mohammad Fakhruddin
**Topic:** Query Fan-Out and Multi-Platform SEO

## 1. Query Fan-Out Strategy
Mohammad explains that search engines no longer look at just one query; they explore the "bigger picture."
- **The Playbook:** Instead of "One Keyword → One Page," move to "One Topic → Multiple Intents." 
- **Execution:** Cover sub-topics and related questions (Query Fan-Out) to build "Topical Authority."

## 2. Social SEO as a Discovery Channel
Your website is no longer your only SEO asset. 
- **Cross-Platform Indexing:** High-value LinkedIn posts can rank in Google and be cited by ChatGPT.
- **AI Recommendation Engine:** If your brand appears consistently across LinkedIn, YouTube, and Reddit, AI gets a better understanding of your expertise, leading to more frequent citations.

## 3. The "Sales Mind" Prompting
To get technical wins, Mohammad advocates for "Prompt Engineering" in SEO:
- Use specific AI prompts for **Schema, Core Web Vitals fixes, and technical site audits.**
- The goal is to move from "Guessing" to "Systematic Heavy Lifting" using AI.
