Everyone's talking about AI. Nobody's actually using it.


Here's where to start:


1. Win new customers on autopilot


Find your ideal customers in Apollo, then drop them into Instantly to send automated cold email sequences. New leads in your inbox on autopilot.


2. Close more deals


Record your sales calls with Fathom, then drop the transcripts into NotebookLM. Ask it where you're losing people and exactly what to say differently next time.


3. Write emails in your voice


Create a Claude skill with your tone, your style, and examples of your best emails. Every email you write from now on sounds like you.


4. Get customers coming to you


Use Kleo to write posts that build your brand and bring inbound leads. No staring at a blank page.


5. Turn customer calls into your roadmap


Record every customer call with Fathom, then paste the transcripts into Claude. Ask "what are customers asking for most?" Build what they actually want, not what you think they want.


6. Create stunning presentations from scratch


Ask ChatGPT to outline your presentation, slide by slide. Paste it into Gamma and it handles all the design. No PowerPoint skills needed.


7. Turn scattered thoughts into a clear plan


Open Claude, switch on voice-to-text, and talk through any problem out loud. It organises your thoughts and tells you exactly what to do next.


8. Get smarter on any topic in 20 minutes


Open Perplexity Deep Research and type "explain [topic] like I'm a beginner, then go deep." It pulls from hundreds of sources and writes you a full breakdown.
